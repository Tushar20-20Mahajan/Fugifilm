A Powerful Performance

Zoom lenses for large format sensors don't come any smaller or lighter than GF35-70mmF4.5-5.6 WR, yet there's no compromise in image quality. Like all other GF lenses, this compact zoom resolves sensors over 100MP, so you can record life in large format and enjoy all the outstanding optical benefits this brings. Your memories will never look so good!

Take Large Format Further

GFX System cameras take the experience of creating images on large format out of the studio and into the world. To enhance this experience further, GF35-70mmF4.5-5.6 WR autofocuses in less than 0.13 seconds* and uses the in-body image stabilization systems in the latest GFX cameras to make it easier for you to work handheld. Focus breathing is also minimized to ensure consistent framing when creating video. *When in the Phase Detection AF mode on the FUJIIFILM GFX100S; Compliant with the CIPA guidelines

A New Normal

The Fujinon legacy of optical excellence is further enhanced by GF35-70mmF4.5-5.6 WR. A truly versatile wide aperture prime lens, this is the first X Mount lens that exactly matches the field of view of an equivalent 50mm focal length – sure to deliver extraordinary performance every day for years to come.
